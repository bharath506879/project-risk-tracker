How This Tool Works

Understand if your project is at risk of failure before it's too late.

What does this do?

This website acts as an early warning system. By looking at how your project is running (like how much time or money is left), it calculates a simple percentage telling you how likely the project is to fail.

Why is it useful?

It helps managers take early action. Instead of being surprised by a failed deadline or a blown budget, you get immediate suggestions on how to save time, reduce costs, and avoid project failure.

How it works (Step-by-Step):

1

Enter Data: You manually type in your project details, OR you paste a GitHub link.

2

System Analyzes: The engine automatically looks at activity, progress, budgets, and open issues.

3

Get Your Score: It calculates a Risk Score (0-100%) and tells you exactly what is causing the risk.

4

Fix the Problem: It gives you clear, actionable suggestions to get your project back on track.

For Developers & Researchers

This interface uses a highly extensible mathematical scoring engine. In future iterations, this deterministic layer can easily be replaced by real machine learning models (like Federated Learning nodes) to analyze historical datasets directly while preserving local data privacy.
